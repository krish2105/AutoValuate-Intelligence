# Web research findings

The web search did not identify a ready-to-use public dataset that simultaneously
meets all of these requirements:

- Captured in UAE product conditions.
- Genuine whole-car framing.
- Bounding-box annotations.
- Exact eight-class order.
- Rights suitable for the intended use.
- Vehicle/session-safe train, validation, and test splits.

Useful but non-equivalent sources were found:

- CarDD: large labelled car-damage dataset, six categories, not UAE-specific, and
  official download access requires a licensing form.
- A YOLO-converted CarDD copy: six categories and non-commercial licensing.
- Humans in the Loop: CC0 damage polygons, but different taxonomy and geography.
- UAE government/Dubai Police open data: tabular incident statistics and
  coordinates, not labelled vehicle photographs.
- UAE auction sites: attractive domain imagery, but site terms and image/data
  licensing make scraping and redistribution inappropriate without permission.

The correct source for this dataset remains the authorised UAE photo pipeline,
field collection, or partner data.
